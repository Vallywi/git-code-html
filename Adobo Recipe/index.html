Ingradients

  • Chicken: Bone-in pieces (thighs, drumsticks) are common
  • Soy Sauce: Adds saltiness and umami
  • Vinegar:White or cane vinegar for tang
  • Garlic: Crushed or minced, essential for flavor.
  • Bay Leaves: For aroma
  • Black Peppercorns: Whole peppercorns for a subtle spice.
